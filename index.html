Welcome to The Columbine Guest House

About Us

Welcome to The Columbine Guest House, your perfect getaway in Swakopmund, Namibia. Our beautiful guest house stands on a 600 square meter plot and offers 3 bedrooms for up to 7 guests. Experience comfort and tranquility in our well-equipped house, situated just 2.5 km from the beach and Platz Am Meer mall.

Rooms & Rates

Our guest house is available for booking as a whole for families or groups. The rates per night are as follows:

  • September, October, November, January, February, March, April: R1900 (€95)
  • December: R2700 (€135)
  • May, June, July, August: R1500 (€75)

Book at least 2 weeks in advance to get a 10% discount. We offer free cancellation up to 1 day before check-in, or a 20% discount if you pay a non-refundable deposit of at least 10% of the price.
